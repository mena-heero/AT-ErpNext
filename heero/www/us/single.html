10 Truths About Marketing

After the Pandemic: This article by Janet Balis from Harvard Business Review discusses how the Covid-19 pandemic has changed the rules of marketing and what marketers need to do to adapt and succeed in the new normal. Some of the key points are: Marketing begins with knowing your customer segment, not just your customer. Marketing is about creating experiences, not just products or services. The Who, What, Why, & How of Digital Marketing

This article by Lucy Alexander from HubSpot Blog explains what digital marketing is, why it is important, and how it can be done effectively. Some of the main topics are: Digital marketing is any form of online marketing that uses digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites to connect with customers. Digital marketing is important because it allows marketers to reach a large and diverse audience, personalize their messages, track their performance, and optimize their campaigns. Digital marketing can be done using various types of tactics and channels, such as content marketing, SEO, SEM, email marketing, social media marketing, video marketing, influencer marketing, and more. Marketing 101: The Ultimate Guide for Beginners: This article by HubSpot Blog provides a comprehensive introduction to the basics of marketing, such as what it is, how it works, and what are the best practices. Some of the essential concepts are: Marketing is the process of creating, delivering, and exchanging value with customers and stakeholders. Marketing works by following a four-step process: understanding the market, developing a strategy, executing the tactics, and evaluating the results. Marketing best practices include setting SMART goals, conducting SWOT analysis, defining your target market and buyer personas, creating a unique selling proposition and value proposition, choosing the right marketing mix (product, price, place, promotion), and measuring your return on investment.
